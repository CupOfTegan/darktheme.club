How do I join The Darktheme Club?

If you're interested in getting your site added to The Darktheme Club, all you need to do is follow these instructions:

  1. Make sure your site offers a dark mode by default, either by simply not having a light mode, through JavaScript or (best) through the prefers-color-scheme media query.
  2. If your site satisfies this requirement, create a pull request (instructions here) to add your site.
  3. I will then review you PR and merge it into main. Once merged, your site will be added to the list.

Note: I reserve the right to not add sites based on whether I think they're suitable to be added or not.
