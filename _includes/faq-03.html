What's the point of all this?

That's a great question!

I decided to start this project for a couple of reasons:

  1. People with certain visual imparements have a hard time navigating a web without a dark theme. There are plugins that try to emulate a dark theme for some sites, but those are not always accurate.
  2. As a software engineer, I often stay up late browsing through articles on HackerNews or lobste.rs. After spending some time in my dark-themed editor and dark-themed search engines, the last thing my eyes want to see is a blindingly bright website preventing my brain from entering "sleep mode".
  3. Most, if not all, of the sites listed are personal sites. Many of which are blogs. The Darktheme Club is a great way of discovering other blog owners who are also interested in accessible web design. Think of it like a modern day webring.
  4. It's a bit of fun, so why not?
